Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my trip?
For popular destinations during peak season, we recommend booking at least 6-8 months in advance. For off-season travel or less popular destinations, 3-4 months is usually sufficient. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible, but options may be limited.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and in some cases, installment payments. A deposit is typically required to secure your booking, with the balance due 60 days before departure.
Do you offer travel insurance?
Yes, we highly recommend travel insurance for all our travelers. We can help arrange comprehensive coverage that includes trip cancellation, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and other unexpected events. Just ask your travel consultant for details.
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions or special needs?
Absolutely! We pride ourselves on crafting personalized experiences. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions, accessibility needs, or other special requirements when booking, and we'll ensure these are communicated to all relevant service providers.
